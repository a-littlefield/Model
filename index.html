Paul W. Koester

The first camera in Paul's possession was a little Kodak Instamatic 104. Though he was only 6 years old at the time, he remembers it well and took it just about everywhere he went. The thrill and anticipation of snapping photos and then taking them to be developed is hard to forget. He would anxiously wait to see what gems that he captured, hoping he captured them well. Though that camera is long gone, the thrill of capturing a moment in a special way has never passed. Source
